Useless Knowledge Trivia

Game Objectives

To start the game, click the Start button. Clicking the start button will begin the clock's countdown, and a series of ten questions will appear. The entire series is timed, so you must answer all 10 questions within the allotted time.

Each question is multiple choice, you will be notified at the end of the game of the count of correct answer, incorrect answers and unanswered answers.

If you reach 10 Correct answers, you can consider yourself an Expert of Useless Knowlege.
