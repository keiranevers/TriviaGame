Useless Knowledge Trivia

Game Objectives

To start the game, clicke the Start button. Clicking the start button will begin the clock's countdown, and a series of ten questions will appear individually. The entire series is timed, so you must answer all 10 questions within the allotted time.

Each question is multiple choice, you will be notified if you have answered correctly or not after your selection, at which time, you may proceed to the next question. Once you answer a question, you will not be given the opportunity to go back to that question to select another answer. The next question will appear for you to choose an answer to.

Each question is worth 10 points, if you reach 100 points, you can win the game as an Expert of Useless Knowlege.
